About the project

VITAL is an Electronic Health Record (EHR) portal built to provide clinicians and patients a unified, privacy-first interface for managing clinical data and enabling early-onset disease prediction using interpretable ML models. The platform integrates:

  • Secure storage of clinical observations and metadata (temperature, bias, waveform etc.).
  • Clinician tools for model-driven risk scoring and decision support.
  • Research-friendly, de-identified export endpoints with authorization controls.

Our goal is to assist early intervention by flagging high-risk individuals for follow-up, thereby improving outcomes and optimizing care resources.

Why early prediction matters

Early detection of chronic diseases enables preventative care, reduces hospitalizations, and lowers treatment cost. Predictive signals — when combined with timely clinical workflows — allow targeted screening and personalized interventions.

Diseases covered

Cardiovascular Disease (CVD)
Predicts risk of coronary events and heart failure. Early detection supports lifestyle and medication interventions that reduce mortality.
Model signals: BP trends, ECG markers, lipids, age, prior history.
Diabetes
Early risk prediction enables targeted screening and glycaemic control measures to prevent complications like neuropathy and retinopathy.
Model signals: fasting glucose, BMI, family history, blood pressure.
Kidney Failure
Early detection of declining renal function helps preserve kidney health and avoids emergency dialysis through timely interventions.
Model signals: creatinine trends, eGFR, blood pressure, medication history.
Liver Function
Flags early liver injury and dysfunction so clinicians can address causes and slow progression toward chronic liver disease.
Model signals: LFTs, alcohol history, metabolic markers.
